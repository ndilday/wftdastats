Explaination of Columns

Exp Share: The portion of the game points FlatTrackStats expects the selected team to get.

Gain: How much above your current WFTDA Rankings Score the expected points share will get you. For example, if the selected team currently has a WFTDA Rankings Score of 300, and the Gain Column lists 20%, that means the predicted victory would have a Rankings Score value of 360. (The actual increase to the team's ranking score would depend on a number of other things, like how many games are in their twelve-month window, whether any are falling out before the next WFTRA Rankings Calculation, etc.)

Opp Gain: Identical to the previous column, but from the opposing team's perspective.
