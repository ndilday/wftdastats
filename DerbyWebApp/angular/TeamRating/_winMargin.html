Win Margin Calculator

Because WFTDA Rankings Scores have been inflating over the past couple of years, it's often useful to understand what is required to achieve a specific score, rather than to compare to a team's current score. This calculator allows you to pick a team and a desired WFTDA Rankings Score for a bout, and lists the point share needed against the teams in the system to achieve that score. It also gives an example score that gives that share.
