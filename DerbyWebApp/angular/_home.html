Welcome to WFTDAStats.com

First things first: I have absolutely no affiliation with WFTDA. I think they're a great organization. I love going to their bouts. I have many friends who play in WFTDA leagues. But I am not a member of any WFTDA league, I do not speak for any WFTDA league, I don't work for WFTDA, etc, etc. This project is a labor of love that I've worked on over many months based on the data made available via the Rinxter API. I commend WFTDA for having the foresight to make their stat data publicly available in that form, and I hope that they find a way to import statbooks into Rinxter, and that their next generation of derby data tools will be just as open and useful to the public.

Currently, there are two main "buckets" of data I'm making available.

The first is my attempt to integrate WFTDA Rankings data with FTS Rankings data (and the math backing each) to give teams better tools for evaluating what matchups make sense for them. I am hoping to eventually extend this to give ranking estimates as bout results come in.

The second, and far more experimental, body of work I'm making available here is my first pass at comparing the impact different players have on the game.
